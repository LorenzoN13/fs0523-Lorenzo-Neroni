Focaccia with caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appertizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart cranberries, and creamybrie. It will become your new favorite holliday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm waeter
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounce sbrie, cutinto cubes (scape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
